### Week 6 Objectives:

- Understand asynchronous processing and system monitoring services on AWS.
- Practice using Amazon SQS, Amazon SNS, and Amazon CloudWatch.
- Solidify knowledge of Serverless architecture before participating in real project development.

### Week 6 Achievements:

- Understand the operating mechanism of Amazon SQS and Dead Letter Queue.

- Know how to send and receive Message using Amazon SQS.

- Understand the role of Amazon SNS in sending notifications between services.

- Use Amazon CloudWatch to monitor Logs, Metrics, and Alarm.

- Have the ability to combine AWS services in a Serverless model.

- Ready to participate in real project development from week 7.

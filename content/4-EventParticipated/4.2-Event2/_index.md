# Reflection on the "Competition Between Groups" Event (20/06/2026)

### Purpose of the Event

- To create an engaging environment for members to learn and interact with one another.
- To reinforce knowledge through a team-based competition.
- To develop teamwork, critical thinking, and quick decision-making skills.
- To strengthen the connection among FCAJ members.

### Event Highlights

The event was organized as a competition involving **eight teams**, where each team worked together to answer questions prepared by the organizers. The questions covered various topics, including technology, AWS, AI, programming, and practical knowledge.

#### 1) Question-and-Answer Competition

- Each team answered a series of questions within a limited time.
- Team members discussed and collaborated to determine the best possible answers.
- The competition was exciting and highly interactive, with all teams demonstrating great enthusiasm and teamwork.

#### 2) Team Collaboration

- Members worked together to analyze each question and agree on the correct answer.
- The activity helped improve communication, teamwork, and task coordination under time constraints.

#### 3) Award Ceremony

- The organizers announced the final results after all rounds were completed.
- The teams with the highest scores received prizes and recognition from the audience.

### What I Learned

- Reviewed and strengthened my knowledge of technology, AWS, and AI.
- Improved my teamwork and collaboration skills.
- Enhanced my ability to think quickly and solve problems under time pressure.
- Learned valuable approaches and strategies from other participating teams.

### My Experience at the Event

- The competition was lively and enjoyable, with the participation of **eight teams**, creating a friendly yet competitive atmosphere.
- It was a great opportunity to connect with other members and gain new knowledge and practical experience.
- The event helped me become more confident in teamwork while improving my critical thinking and quick response skills.
- I truly enjoyed participating in this activity and hope to join more events like this in the future.

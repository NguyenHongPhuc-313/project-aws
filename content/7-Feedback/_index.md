### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. FCAJ members are always ready to help whenever I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, which helps me focus better. However, I believe it would be good to add more social gatherings or team bonding activities so everyone can get to know each other better.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor gives very detailed guidance, explains clearly when I don’t understand, and always encourages me to ask questions. The admin team supports paperwork and documentation, and creates favorable conditions for me to work effectively. I appreciate that the mentor allows me to try solving problems myself instead of simply providing the answers.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks assigned to me matched the knowledge I learned at university while also exposing me to new areas I had not encountered before. This allowed me to both reinforce my foundational knowledge and gain practical skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, working in a team, and communicating professionally in a corporate environment. The mentor also shared valuable real-world experience that helped me better orient my future career.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects one another, works seriously, but still maintains a pleasant atmosphere. When urgent projects arise, everyone strives together and supports one another regardless of their position. This made me feel like a true part of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provides an internship allowance and offers flexible working hours when necessary. In addition, participating in internal training sessions is a major plus.
